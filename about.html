Our Student Journey

We're a group of computer science students who struggled with maintaining good habits while balancing academics, work, and personal life. We built HabitBuddy to solve this problem for ourselves and others.

Born from late-night coding and passion for personal growth, HabitBuddy is your free, student-friendly tool to build better habits.

Built by students, for students (and everyone else too!)
